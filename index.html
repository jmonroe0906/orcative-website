Featured Platform

Introducing Sonaira by Orcative

Care Navigation · AI Concierge

Proactive care navigation for complex patients before it turns emergent.

Sonaira is an AI care navigation platform designed for self-funded health plans, TPAs, and regional health plans. Purpose-built for commercially insured members with complex medical histories, it guides them to the right path of care, connecting them with rated in-network specialists, facilitating appointments, and providing personalized advocacy every step of the way.

Sonaira empowers members and plan employees to become true healthcare advocates, equipped with the right information at the right moment to make confident care decisions.

How Sonaira works
1
Personalized care pathwaySonaira identifies the right path of care for each member's unique clinical situation. No generic guidance.
2
Rated specialists & providersMembers are matched with rated, in-network specialists suited to their condition and plan coverage.
3
Appointment connectionSonaira connects members directly, removing the friction between guidance and action.
4
Appointment advocacyMembers receive preparation guidance so they walk in informed and empowered, not overwhelmed.
5
Follow-up & continued guidanceAfter care is received, Sonaira stays engaged, supporting next steps and ongoing navigation.
📉
Measurable plan savingsFewer unnecessary claims, avoided ER visits, and reduced out-of-pocket surprises for members and health plans alike.

Featured Platform · Care Navigation

Sonaira by Orcative — AI Care Navigation for Self-Funded Health Plans

A care navigation platform designed for commercially insured members with complex medical histories, guiding them through the right path of care proactively, personally, and completely. Deployed for self-funded health plans, TPAs, and regional health plans.

Key Value Props

  • ✓ Designed for complex patients who don't fit standard care pathways
  • ✓ Guides members to rated, in-network specialists matched to their condition
  • ✓ Connects members directly to schedule appointments, removing friction between guidance and action
  • ✓ Equips members with advocacy tools before, during, and after care
  • ✓ Provides follow-up guidance to support ongoing navigation
  • ✓ Measurably reduces unnecessary utilization and downstream claims cost for self-funded plans
  • ✓ A genuine differentiator for benefits brokers and benefits consultants

How it works

1
Care pathway guidance

Sonaira identifies the right path of care for each member's unique clinical situation. No generic protocols.

2
Specialist matching

Members are matched with rated, in-network providers suited to their specific condition and plan coverage.

3
Appointment connection

Sonaira connects members directly to schedule, removing the gap between knowing what to do and doing it.

4
Appointment advocacy

Members receive preparation guidance so they arrive informed, equipped, and empowered to advocate for themselves.

5
Follow-up & continued navigation

After care is received, Sonaira stays engaged, supporting next steps and keeping members on the right path.
