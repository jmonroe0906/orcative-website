Featured Platform

Introducing Orcative Pathwell

Care Navigation · AI Concierge

Proactive care guidance for complex patients before it turns emergent.

Pathwell is a care navigation platform designed for self-funded health plans, TPAs, and regional health plans. Purpose-built for members with complex medical histories, it guides them to the right path of care, connecting them with rated specialists, facilitating appointments, and providing personalized advocacy every step of the way.

Pathwell empowers members and plan employees to become true healthcare advocates, equipped with the right information at the right moment to make confident care decisions.

How Pathwell works
1
Personalized care pathwayPathwell identifies the right path of care for each member's unique situation. No generic guidance.
2
Rated specialists & providersMembers are matched with in-network, rated specialists suited to their condition and coverage.
3
Appointment connectionPathwell connects members directly, removing the friction between guidance and action.
4
Appointment advocacyMembers receive preparation guidance so they walk in informed and empowered, not overwhelmed.
5
Follow-up & continued guidanceAfter care is received, Pathwell stays engaged, supporting next steps and ongoing navigation.

Featured Platform · Care Navigation

Orcative Pathwell

A care navigation platform designed for commercially insured members with complex medical histories, guiding them through the right path of care proactively, personally, and completely. Deployed for self-funded health plans, TPAs, and regional health plans.

Key Value Props

  • ✓ Designed for complex patients who don't fit standard care pathways
  • ✓ Guides members to rated, in-network specialists matched to their condition
  • ✓ Connects members directly to schedule appointments, removing friction between guidance and action
  • ✓ Equips members with advocacy tools before, during, and after care
  • ✓ Provides follow-up guidance to support ongoing navigation
  • ✓ Measurably reduces unnecessary utilization and downstream claims cost
  • ✓ A genuine differentiator for benefits brokers and consultants

How it works

1
Care pathway guidance

Pathwell identifies the right path of care for each member's unique clinical situation. No generic protocols.

2
Specialist matching

Members are matched with rated, in-network providers suited to their specific condition and plan coverage.

3
Appointment connection

Pathwell connects members directly to schedule, removing the gap between knowing what to do and doing it.

4
Appointment advocacy

Members receive preparation guidance so they arrive informed, equipped, and empowered to advocate for themselves.

5
Follow-up & continued navigation

After care is received, Pathwell stays engaged, supporting next steps and keeping members on the right path.
